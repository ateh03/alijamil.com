ABOUT ME

INTRO

I’m the co-founder of Glint, where I’m exploring how we verify personhood in a post-AI world. Previously, I founded LIQ, a mobile beverage startup that gained traction during a pre-accelerator program before sunsetting due to logistical constraints.

My background spans public sector innovation, data systems, and startup development. I'm currently pursuing my Master’s in Technology Innovation, where I focus on building tools for more intentional human connection.

GERMANE HISTORY

  • As a military child, I lived in Germany, Japan, and Bahrain before age 16. I've now visited 18 countries across 4 continents.
  • At 17, I took AP CSP and started dreaming up solutions in pseudocode.
  • At 18, I watched Dynasty and realized I wanted to be a technopreneur.
  • In 2024, I led my first pre-seed startup and pitched to real investors.
