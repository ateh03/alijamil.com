INTRO

I’m the co-founder of Nexus Technologies, where I’m building interactive matchmaking experiences that merge gaming, dating, and social discovery. Previously, I founded LIQ, a mobile alcoholic beverage delivery concept that completed a pre-accelerator program and received early user traction before shutting down due to logistical constraints.

Before that, I led data analytics initiatives at the South Carolina Department of Employment and Workforce, building a wage data analysis dashboard used by economists and labor analysts. My experience spans public sector innovation, data and database management, system and network security, early-stage startup development, and information technology project management.

I’m currently completing my Master’s in Technology Innovation, and I spend most of my time ideating and designing systems that make human connection more intentional. If you’re working on something bold, feel free to reach out! I’m always open to ideas, collaboration, and conversations that challenge the norm.
